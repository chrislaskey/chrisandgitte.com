The ceremony and reception site at Spruce Point Inn is located close to downtown Boothbay Harbor. There are plenty of places to stay in the surrounding area at a variety of price points.

We will be in Boothbay Harbor for Labor Day weekend and welcome you to enjoy Maine for the long weekend.

Staying at Spruce Point Inn

Amenities at Spruce Point Inn include saltwater and freshwater pools, clay tennis courts, fitness room, yoga, hiking trails, kayaks, bicycles, shuffleboard, complementary harbor tours, and hammocks. Spruce Point Inn is a luxury resort and prices are on the high end compared to other accommodations in Boothbay Harbor.

Spruce Point Inn
http://www.sprucepointinn.com/

We have reserved a block of rooms at Spruce Point Inn at a discounted rate for our guests. The rooms vary in size and price but all have a 3 night minimum stay. To reserve one of these rooms or find out more about them, please contact Cindy Poe (207-633-4152 or cpoe@sprucepointinn.com). In order to take advantage of the discount you must book by May 4th.
