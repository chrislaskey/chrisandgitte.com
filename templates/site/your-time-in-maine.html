Staying at Spruce Point Inn

We have reserved a block of rooms at Spruce Point Inn at a discounted rate for our guests. The rooms vary in size and price. To reserve one of these room or find out more about them, please contact Cindy Poe (207-633-4152 or CPoe@sprucepointinn.com).

Spruce Point Inn http://www.sprucepointinn.com/

Spruce Point Inn has a 3 night minimum stay. Amenities include saltwater and freshwater pools, clay tennis courts, fitness room, yoga, hiking trails, kayaks, bicycles, shuffleboard, complementary harbor tours, and hammocks.
